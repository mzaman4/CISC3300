Issues surrounding the web

Long Live the Web" by Tim Berners-Lee, is an article that discussed the web and what factors have threatened the evolution of the web.

Topics covered:

  • Universality
  • Open Standards
  • Seperation of Web and Internet
  • Human Rights
  • Snooping

The first topic that caught my attention was Universality. I was made aware of the issue on how social media platforms can stagnate web growth by withholding user data.
I always viewed the web as a collection of websites, where each website was it's own seperate entity. However this article made me realize that this is not the case.
Websites contributing to the growth of the web as a whole is an interesting concept.

Human rights is another topic that caught my attention. Specifically net nuetrality. As we all know, net nuetrality was repealed. It is very sad how this fear existed in 2010
and yet the people who voted for the repeal did not understand how important net nuetrality is, not only as a right but also as a neccesity to improve and maintain the web.
